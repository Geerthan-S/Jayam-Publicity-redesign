Step 01

Discovery Call

We start by listening. Understanding your brand, goals, target audience, budget, and the market pressure you're navigating.

  • Brand background, product or service positioning
  • Target geography, demographics, and psychographics
  • Campaign objective — awareness, launches, recall, footfall
  • Budget range and decision timeline
  • Past campaign history and what worked or didn't
Typically 30–60 min call or meeting
01
02

Step 02

Media Strategy

We match your objective to the right formats, locations, routes, and timing — no bloated plans, only what drives practical reach.

  • Format selection — hoardings, bus shelters, medians, transit, digital
  • Location scouting and site shortlisting with footfall data
  • Route planning for mobile and transit formats
  • Timing and duration recommendations by format
  • Budget allocation across formats with reach estimates
Proposal delivered within 3–5 working days

Step 03

Creative & Production

Artwork coordination, print production, material procurement, and vendor alignment — we handle the complexity so you don't have to.

  • Artwork review for print-readiness and format compliance
  • Print production coordination — flex, vinyl, backlit, digital
  • Material procurement and quality checks
  • Vendor and sub-contractor briefing and supervision
  • Proof approval workflow with client sign-off
5–10 working days depending on format
03
04

Step 04

Installation & Launch

On-ground installation with photo documentation and a launch confirmation report delivered to you on Day 1.

  • Site preparation and permission verification
  • Supervised installation by trained ground crew
  • Geo-tagged photo documentation of all sites
  • Installation completion report with timestamps
  • Client walkthrough or remote confirmation
Installation Day — report same day

Step 05

Live Monitoring

Campaign tracking, periodic site visits, condition checks, and issue resolution throughout the campaign duration.

  • Scheduled site visits and condition audits
  • Material integrity checks — weather, wear, vandalism
  • Rapid issue resolution and re-installation if needed
  • Mid-campaign photo updates shared with client
  • Route tracking for mobile van campaigns
Ongoing throughout campaign duration
05
06

Step 06

Reporting & Review

Post-campaign analytics, reach estimates, proof-of-performance material, and an ROI discussion to plan what comes next.

  • Full photo proof-of-performance documentation
  • Reach and impression estimates by format and location
  • Campaign timeline and milestone summary
  • ROI discussion and benchmark comparison
  • Next campaign recommendations based on outcomes
Report delivered within 5 days of campaign end
